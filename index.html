Biography

Yifan is currently a PhD student working on computational social science in School of Business and Management in Queen Mary University of London, United Kingdom. His current research is broadly concerned with network science, graph theory, deep learning and data science under the supervision of Dr. Pietro Panzarasa and Prof. Pedro Martins.

Before coming to London, he has obtained his BSc and MSc degree at Sino-French Engineer School in Beihang University in China, majoring in computer science. He has also studied abroad as exchange student for two years (including the last year of BSc and the first year of MSc) at Ecole Centrale de Nantes in France within the engineering programme. At Centrale Nantes, he followed one year of the common-core engineering syllabus and one year of specialisation (computer science). At the end of the programme, he graduated with the Centrale Nantes Diplôme d’ingénieur - equivalent to a Master’s Degree in Engineering - in addition to the degree of his home institution.

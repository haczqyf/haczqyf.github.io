Biography

Yifan is currently a PhD researcher working on computational social science. His current research is broadly concerned with network science, data science and machine learning under the supervision of Dr. Pietro Panzarasa and Prof. Pedro Martins.

Before coming to London, he has obtained his BSc and MSc degree at Sino-French Engineer School in Beihang University in China, majoring in computer science. He has also studied abroad as exchange student for two years (including the last year of BSc and the first year of MSc) at Ecole Centrale de Nantes in France, majoring in computer science.
